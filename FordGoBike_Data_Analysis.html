Ford GoBike System Data Analysis¶

Introduction¶

This notebook explores the Ford GoBike System Data to answer the following business questions:

  1. What are the peak times and days for bike usage? Understanding peak usage times can help in optimizing bike distribution and station management.
  2. How does bike usage vary by user type and gender? Analyzing bike usage across different user demographics can guide targeted marketing strategies and service improvements.
  3. What are the most common start and end stations, and how does this impact the overall efficiency of the bike-sharing system? Identifying popular stations can help in optimizing bike placement and ensuring that high-demand areas are well-served.

The goal is to prepare the data, analyze it, and provide insights that address these questions.

The high usage of certain stations can lead to imbalances in bike availability and demand. Stations with consistently high demand may experience shortages, while stations with consistently low demand may have excess bikes. This can impact the overall efficiency of the bike-sharing system.

To improve efficiency, the bike-sharing operator could consider:
    Redistributing bikes from stations with excess bikes to stations with high demand.
    Implementing dynamic pricing to encourage usage at less popular stations.
    Expanding the number of bikes and stations in high-demand areas
